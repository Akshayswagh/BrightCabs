OUR SERVICES

Our Services

Employee Transportation

We specialize in safe, timely, and technology-driven employee commute solutions for corporates, BPOs, and IT/ITES companies. Our operations are backed by real-time tracking, automated scheduling, and a 24/7 support system to ensure employees reach work and return home comfortably and securely—every single day.

Corporate Car Rental

Bright Cabs offers a fleet of well-maintained sedans, SUVs, and premium cars tailored to suit business travel needs. Whether it's executive pick-ups, inter-office travel, or client meetings, our chauffeured services are designed to reflect professionalism, punctuality, and class.

Airport Transfers

We provide reliable and punctual airport transportation for business executives and employees. Our airport transfer services include both drop and pick-up options, with live flight tracking to ensure minimal wait time and a smooth travel experience.

Long-Term Car Rental

For businesses that require dedicated vehicles for extended durations, we offer flexible long-term rental plans with or without chauffeurs. These solutions are ideal for executives, project-based staff, or company directors who require consistent, on-call mobility.

Event Transportation

We offer end-to-end transportation planning and execution for corporate events, conferences, team outings, and VIP movements. Our fleet and coordination team ensure hassle-free logistics so your event attendees arrive on time, every time.

Electric Vehicle (EV) Transportation

In support of sustainable mobility, Bright Cabs is proud to offer Electric Vehicle (EV) options across multiple service categories. Our EV fleet reduces carbon footprint while delivering the same comfort, efficiency, and reliability expected from our brand—making your company’s transportation both eco-conscious and future-ready.
