About us

Bright Cabs

Bright Urban Mobility Private Limited, popularly known as Bright Cabs, is a trusted name in corporate commute and employee transportation services since 1997. Formerly known as Om Sai Travels, established in 1997 and reconstituted as Bright Urban Mobility Private Limited in the year of 2022.
With over two decades of experience, we have been at the forefront of transforming how organizations move their people safely, punctually, and efficiently.

Headquartered in Kharadi, Pune, Maharashtra, Bright Cabs specializes in providing customized transport solutions to leading corporates, IT/ITES companies, and business parks across India. Our commitment to safety, reliability, and round-the-clock service has earned us long-standing partnerships with some of the most respected names in the industry.

From deploying GPS-enabled fleets to offering real-time ride tracking and automated rostering, we combine technology with dependable service to ensure seamless mobility for employees and peace of mind for our clients.
At Bright Cabs, we believe in sustainable and smart mobility. Our fleet includes a mix of sedans, MUVs, and electric vehicles to support green transportation initiatives.

Bright Cabs Services

Spot Rentals

Spot rentals refer to renting vehicles for short periods or on the spot, often without a prior reservation. These rentals are ideal for immediate needs, such as emergencies, sudden trips, or when local transportation options are limited. They typically involve quick paperwork and are available at rental counters or through mobile apps. Spot rentals offer flexibility in terms of rental duration, vehicle type selection, and return options, making them convenient for spontaneous travel plans or unexpected situations.

Monthly Leased Rentals

Monthly leased rentals provide individuals and businesses with access to vehicles for extended periods, typically ranging from one to twelve months. These arrangements offer flexibility in vehicle selection, from economy cars to SUVs or luxury models, catering to diverse needs. They often include benefits such as maintenance services and comprehensive insurance coverage, simplifying the lessee’s responsibilities. Monthly leases are cost-effective compared to short-term rentals and provide stability in transportation needs without the commitment of vehicle ownership.

Long Term Rentals

Long-term rentals refer to vehicle leasing agreements that extend beyond traditional short-term rental periods, typically spanning several months to a few years. These rentals are advantageous for individuals and businesses requiring consistent and reliable transportation without the upfront costs and commitments of vehicle ownership. They offer flexibility in vehicle selection, often including options for economy cars, vans, or specialty vehicles tailored to specific needs. Long-term rentals commonly include maintenance services and comprehensive insurance coverage, providing convenience and peace of mind throughout the lease duration.
